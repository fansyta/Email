TENANT SPOTLIGHT - 100% PURE

100% Pure, a local, organic cosmetic company, was founded in 2005 by Susie Wang. Susie’s desire was to invent a line of products that contained natural ingredients. Cosmetic companies were immediately interested in the products and Susie continued to develop new formulas. One fateful evening, while formulating an eye cream, she accidently spilled a key ingredient and the potent chemical completely eroded her lab table. Shocked by the ingredient’s toxicity, Susie became passionate about creating a line that was free of harsh detergents, artificial fragrances, chemical preservatives or any other toxins.

Today, 100% Pure offers more than 500 products in the following categories:  fruit pigmented cosmetics, skincare, bath & body products and hair care and has the healthiest line of products without synthetic chemicals, artificial ingredients, petro chemicals, chemical preservatives and toxins. All products are 100% biodegradable and packaged with recycled materials.  

100% Pure products have been used by celebrities including Jessica Alba, Kate Hudson and Reese Witherspoon. Stop by 100% Pure in Terminal 3 near Gate 74, and take advantage of the generous 50% discount that is available to SFO employees. 

For more information, visit http://www.100percentpure.com/.

NEWS AROUND THE AIRPORT

SFO Sets Record Passenger Traffic in 2012


SFO set an all-time record for passenger traffic in 2012, with a total of 44.5 million passengers traveling through SFO. This surpasses the previous record of 41 million passengers set in 2011, and represents an 8.5% increase in traffic year-over-year.

SFO continues to be a vital source for job creation and contribution to local economies. The airport generated a total of 29,555 direct jobs and $1.7 billion in personal income. SFO also generated $469 million in state and local taxes. Construction projects launched during 2012 continue to add jobs to the area.

SFO offers a wide variety of travel options to passengers with 30 international carriers serving 31 international points, as well as 15 domestic airlines offering nonstop service to 74 cities in the U.S. In the Bay Area, SFO accounts for two-thirds of all domestic flight activity, and continues to serve as the primary international gateway.

SFO Wins Multiple Awards for Concession Program

  Airport Revenue News (ARN) honored SFO with four awards for outstanding concession programs. In its annual Best Airport & Concession Awards Program, SFO won for “Airport With the Best Concessions Management Team”, and tied for “Airport With the Best Overall Concession Program” in the Large Airport category.  In the Single Terminal Division, SFO’s Terminal 2 was recognized as the “Airport With the Most Unique Services” and “Airport With the Best Overall Concession Program.”

"The entire team at SFO is proud to be recognized as an industry leader,” said Airport Director John L. Martin.  “Our commitment to create a world-class experience for guests is validated by these awards.”

SFO thanks all of the concession tenants who made these awards possible.  We could not have done it without you!

Thank You Pacific Gateway Concessions

As part of fulfilling the Airport’s mission of providing an exceptional airport in service to our communities, SFO has provided Airport Commission staff with opportunities to volunteer and donate items to several non-profits. Last December, a group of employees worked closely with Project Homeless Connect (PHC), a non-profit organization committed to providing access to essential services to San Francisco's homeless.

After identifying PHC's needs, the Committee organized a donation drive to collect hoodies to clothe San Francisco's homeless. The Airport Commission recently recognized the staff's philanthropic work by awarding its inaugural SFO Service to Communities Award to the Holiday Hoodie Drive Team.

Pacific Gateway Concessions (PGC) partnered with SFO by generously donating 200 new sweatshirts. Airport employees donated an additional 175 hoodies and $1,104 in cash, which was used to purchase additional sweatshirts for the PHC clients. SFO thanks PGC for its partnership on this project and its donation to PHC!

OPERATIONS CORNER

SFO Welcomes Eva Cheong

SFO welcomes Ms. Eva Cheong, Associate Deputy Airport Director of Airport Services in the Operations and Security Division. Ms. Cheong oversees the Airport Services groups which include AirTrain Operations, Duty Management, Landside Operations and Wayfinding. 

Ms. Cheong brings a wide breadth of experience to the Airport having worked with Delta Air Lines for the past 18 years.  In 1994, Ms. Cheong's airline career began when she was a Customer Service Agent in San Diego.  She was then promoted to Station Manager at both SFO and OAK.  Most recently, as Field Director, Ms. Cheong was responsible for managing operations at seven airports and more than 100 daily flights.  Ms. Cheong received her B.A. from the University of Michigan.

AIRLINE FLIGHT ARRIVALS AND DEPARTURES 

SAS Launches Non-Stop Service to Copenhagen

 On April 8, 2013, Scandinavian Airlines (SAS) will launch the first ever non-stop service to Copenhagen, Denmark. This flight will connect SFO and the Bay Area to Scandinavia, Finland, Russia, the Baltic, Poland and Germany.

SAS will depart from San Francisco to Copenhagen daily, except Tuesdays, at 5:35 p.m. arriving the next day in Copenhagen at 1:15 p.m. Departures from Copenhagen will be daily, except Tuesdays  at 12:25 p.m., arriving San Francisco at 2:45 p.m. on the same day.

For more information, visit: http://www.flysas.com/en/us/. 

China Eastern Commences Non-Stop Service to Shanghai

 On April 26, China Eastern Airlines will commence non-stop service from SFO to Shanghai (PVG). The flight will depart SFO at 12:00 noon and arrive in Shanghai at 4:00 p.m. the following day. The return flight will depart Shanghai at 1:00 p.m. and will arrive in San Francisco at 9:30 a.m. on the same day.

For more information, visit us.flychinaeastern.com.

FOR AIRPORT FAMILIES

Palcare is a unique child care and early education center near SFO in Burlingame. Designed with SFO families in mind, Palcare offers flexible, nurturing education and care in an environment that supports the growth and development of children, their families, and the care-giving professionals that work with them. Palcare provides childcare for infants (up to 24 months), toddlers (24-36 months) and Preschoolers (3 years to Kindergarten) and is open from 6:30 a.m. to 11:00 p.m., Monday through Saturday.

Priority for enrollment is given to children of airport employees. For more information, visit www.palcare.org, call 650.340.1289 x311 or email information@palcare.org.
