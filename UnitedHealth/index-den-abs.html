Join us for dinner and dialogue about individual and community preparedness for Denver’s growing aging population. This event, hosted by Denver Regional Council of Governments Area Agency on Aging, the National Association of Area Agencies on Aging, the National Council on Aging and UnitedHealthcare, will bring together a select group of influential local leaders to review the Denver-specific results of the United States of Aging Survey and discuss opportunities to support Denver’s older Americans.

We look forward to seeing you at Elway’s for a lively discussion.

Please note this invitation is non-transferable. If you are unable to attend and would like to send another member of your organization, please reach out to USofAging@bm.com.
